Natural Language Processing (NLP) combines computer science, AI, and the study of language

Human language, as complex as it is, can be understood by computers through NLP. They can even generate human language in ways we had seen only in futuristic movies!

In this decade, NLP has become part of everyday life in the digital world. It's present in customer service chatbots, text translation, image generation, GPS systems, and more

NLP has become an important tool for automating certain digital duties and collecting insight

Though there are challenges, NLP is evolving fast in making machine-human communication more natural
