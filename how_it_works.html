Natural Language Processing (NLP) combines computational methods to understand and generate human language.

While its a complex process, here's an overview of the common NLP pipeline:

1. Collect text from sources and store it in structured formats

2. Clean and prepare text with:

  • Tokenization (splitting into words/sentences)
  • Removing lowercases, punctuation, and stopwords
  • Reducing words to their base forms
  • Normalization (fixing contractions, spelling fixes, special characters)

3. Convert text into numerical form

4. Analyze patterns and context

5. Use machine learning

6. Use trained models for tasks like classification, translation, Q&A, sentiment analysis, etc.

7. Measure the performance and look for errors
